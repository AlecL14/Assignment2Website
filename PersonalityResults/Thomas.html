Thomas's Personal Profile

Myers-Briggs Test

The results from the Myers-Briggs test indicate that I am by far more turbulent than I am assertive. I see this as a negative, especially in a group scenario as it often means that I lack leadership skills and often don’t have as much of an impact in pushing the group in a certain direction. It’s important to have members in my team that portray the traits that are somewhat absent from myself.

Big Five Personality Trait

Out of all the tests, the big five personality test is one that will be most useful to use when in a group scenario. This test suggests that I am possess more conscientiousness and am emotionally stable. This suggests I am a hard worker who is able to work under pressure – an important trait to have, especially when working in a group.

Learning Styles

This test tells me that I am mostly a reflector and theorist style learner. I learn by observing actions of others and by reading into topics to better understand them. In a team environment, I will learn better by watching my peers perform tasks which can be accompanied with extra reading if necessary.
